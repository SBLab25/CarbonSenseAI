# CarbonSense AI Design System

## Aesthetic Overview
A refined, modern, and colourful dashboard application that feels premium, professional, and engaging. The design should draw inspiration from high-end enterprise SaaS and modern productivity tools, incorporating clean typography, subtle glassmorphism, structured card layouts, and vibrant accents.

## Theme & Colors
- **Primary Color**: Emerald Green (`#10b981`) representing the eco-friendly focus, used for primary actions, active states, and highlights.
- **Secondary Accent**: Sapphire Blue (`#3b82f6`) or Indigo (`#6366f1`) for secondary elements, charts, and information.
- **Background (Light Mode)**: Soft off-white or very light gray (`#f8fafc` or `#f3f4f6`) for the main canvas.
- **Background (Dark Mode)**: Deep charcoal or slate (`#0f172a` or `#1e293b`) for the canvas.
- **Surface/Cards**: Pure white (`#ffffff`) in light mode, and elevated slate (`#1e293b` or `#334155`) in dark mode with subtle borders (`1px solid #e2e8f0` / `#334155`) and soft, diffused shadows.

## Typography
- **Font Family**: 'Inter', 'Outfit', or a similar modern sans-serif.
- **Headings**: Semi-bold to bold weights with tight letter spacing.
- **Body**: Clean, readable sizes (14px to 16px) with adequate line-height (1.5).

## Layout Structure
1. **Sidebar Navigation**:
   - A distinct sidebar, either a deep solid color (like dark blue/slate) OR a light sidebar with strong active-state highlighting.
   - Clean icons paired with text labels.
   - User profile section pinned to the bottom or top.
2. **Main Content Area**:
   - Tabbed navigation where applicable (e.g., separating details, overview, settings).
   - "Bento-box" style grid layouts for dashboards, utilizing rounded corners (e.g., `rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`).
3. **Top Navigation/Header**:
   - Minimalist, holding page title, breadcrumbs, search, and notification/action icons.

## Components & Elements
- **Cards & Containers**: Should have padding (e.g., `p-6`), rounded corners (`rounded-xl`), and subtle drop shadows (`shadow-sm` or `shadow-md`).
- **Inputs & Forms**: Rounded inputs (`rounded-lg`), subtle borders, soft focus rings (e.g., `focus:ring-2 focus:ring-emerald-500`).
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid background (Emerald or Blue), rounded, hover effects.
  - Secondary/Outlined: Transparent background with colored border.
  - Toggles/Switches: Pill-shaped, smooth transitions.
- **Data Visualization**:
  - Use vibrant but harmonious colors for charts (emerald, teal, blue, amber).
  - Clear labels and legends.

## Micro-interactions & Polish
- Hover states on all interactive elements (buttons lifting slightly, links changing color).
- Subtle glassmorphism (backdrop blur) on modal overlays or floating navigation bars.
- Use of badges and pills for statuses (e.g., "Active", "Completed", "New") with soft tinted backgrounds and strong text colors (e.g., light green bg with dark green text).
